Upload Manager

There are many ways to add media to Jinzora. One of these that we've included for you is the Jinzora Upload Manager.

Uploading Uploading media is simple. To access the Upload Manager you must be logged in as an Admin in Jinzora or have specific upload rights. When logged in with admin privs click on "Media Management" then on "Add Media".

The upload manager is very simple and allows for you to upload up to 5 tracks at a time or allows for hte upload of zip files to simplify uploading entire albums.
