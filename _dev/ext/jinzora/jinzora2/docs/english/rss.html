The Jinzora RSS Newsfeeds

There are several RSS Newsfeeds that are built into Jinzora. They are:

  • topplayalbum (played-albums)
  • topplayartist (played-artists)
  • topdownalbum (downloaded-albums)
  • newalbums (new-albums)
  • newartists (new-artists)
  • recentplaytrack (recentplayed-tracks)
  • topplaytrack (played-tracks)
  • recentplayalbum (recentplayed-albums)
  • recentplayartist (recentplayed-artists)
  • topratedalbum (toprated-albums)
  • topratedartist (toprated-artists)
  • topviewartist (topviewed-artists)


    • To use these newsfeed simply point your reader to the location where Jinzora is installed, but instead of pointing to index.html point to rss.php?type=XXX (where XXX is the type of feed you'd like to see, as defined above in the parathenses).
